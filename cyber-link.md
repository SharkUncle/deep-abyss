# Sites that can be used to reveal hidden dangers

## Breach & Credential Exposure
- [HaveIBeenPwned](https://haveibeenpwned.com) - Checks if your data was ever leaked
- [DeHashed](https://dehashed.com) - Searches billions of leaked credentials instantly
- [HudsonRock](https://hudsonrock.com) - Checks if your device appears in infostealer logs

## Malware & File Analysis
- [MalwareBazaar](https://bazaar.abuse.ch) - Analyzes real malware samples completely safely
- [Any.run](https://any.run) - Executes suspicious files inside safe sandbox
- [Hybrid Analysis](https://www.hybrid-analysis.com) - Detonates malware samples revealing hidden behavior
- [VirusTotal](https://www.virustotal.com) - Scans any file or URL against 70 engines

## Phishing & Malicious URLs
- [URLhaus](https://urlhaus.abuse.ch) - Tracks malicious URLs distributing malware live
- [PhishTank](https://www.phishtank.com) - Community database of verified phishing websites
- [OpenPhish](https://openphish.com) - Tracks active phishing URLs targeting users now
- [URLVoid](https://www.urlvoid.com) - Cross checks any suspicious URL against dozens of blacklists

## IP & Threat Intelligence
- [AbuseIPDB](https://www.abuseipdb.com) - Community blacklist reporting malicious IP addresses
- [GreyNoise](https://www.greynoise.io) - Separates real attacks from internet background noise
- [Pulsedive](https://pulsedive.com) - Community threat intelligence aggregating global feeds
- [Talostelligence](https://talosintelligence.com) - Cisco real-time global IP threat reputation database
- [IPVoid](https://www.ipvoid.com) - Cross checks any IP against multiple global blacklists
- [AlienVault OTX](https://otx.alienvault.com) - Community sharing real-time global threat intelligence
- [ThreatFox](https://threatfox.abuse.ch) - Searches and shares malware indicators of compromise

## Internet Exposure & Attack Surface
- [Shodan](https://www.shodan.io) - Finds every dangerously exposed device on internet
- [Censys](https://censys.io) - Maps every misconfigured server exposed online globally
- [BinaryEdge](https://www.binaryedge.io) - Scans entire internet revealing live attack surface
- [ZoomEye](https://www.zoomeye.org) - Exposes hidden vulnerable systems across entire internet
- [FullHunt](https://fullhunt.io) - Discovers every internet-exposed asset on any domain
- [LeakIX](https://leakix.net) - Finds misconfigured servers and exposed databases live
- [GrayhatWarfare](https://grayhatwarfare.com) - Searches millions of exposed public cloud storage

## Exploits & Vulnerability Research
- [ExploitDB](https://www.exploit-db.com) - Database of every publicly known software exploit
- [GTFOBins](https://gtfobins.github.io) - Shows how attackers abuse legitimate Linux binaries
- [LOLBAS](https://lolbas-project.github.io) - Reveals how Windows binaries get weaponized against you
- [Shodan Exploits](https://exploits.shodan.io) - Finds known vulnerabilities for any software version

## Reconnaissance & OSINT
- [SpiderFoot](https://www.spiderfoot.net) - Automates complete digital footprint reconnaissance instantly
- [Grabify](https://grabify.link) - Reveals exact device and location behind any click

## Honeypots & Deception
- [Canarytokens](https://canarytokens.org) - Plants invisible tripwires detecting unauthorized access

## Email & DNS Security
- [MXToolbox](https://mxtoolbox.com) - Diagnoses email servers used in phishing attacks
- [DNSLeakTest](https://www.dnsleaktest.com) - Confirms whether your DNS requests expose location

## VPN & Privacy
- [IPLeak](https://ipleak.net) - Tests whether your VPN actually leaks your identity

## Browser Fingerprinting
- [Creepjs](https://abrahamjuliot.github.io/creepjs) - Reveals how uniquely fingerprinted your browser is
- [WhatIsMyBrowser](https://www.whatismybrowser.com) - Reveals everything websites silently collect about you
- [AmIUnique](https://amiunique.org) - Checks if your browser fingerprint is truly unique

## Network & Packet Analysis
- [PacketTotal](https://packettotal.com) - Analyzes any network capture file for threats
